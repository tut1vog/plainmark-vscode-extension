# Math sampler

Inline and block math without `\tag` or `\label` (those emit per-document numbered IDs that drift snapshots).

## Inline

Pythagoras: $x^2 + y^2 = z^2$.

A fraction inline: $\frac{a}{b}$ inside prose.

A sum inline: $\sum_{i=1}^n i$ in context.

Constant: $\pi$ has a value.

## Block

A fraction:

$$
\frac{a}{b}
$$

An integral:

$$
\int_0^1 x^2 \, dx
$$

A sum with limits:

$$
\sum_{i=1}^{n} i^2
$$

An aligned environment:

$$
\begin{align}
a &= b + c \\
d &= e + f
\end{align}
$$
